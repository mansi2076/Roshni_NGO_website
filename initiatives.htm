EDUCATION FOR ALL



Education makes people easy to lead, but difficult to drive, easy to govern but impossible to enslave.

” Without education, the training of the human mind is incomplete. …

The human mind was made to be trained and without education, a person is incomplete”.

Under this program, Roshni (Sanstha) used to conducts free regular classes for students under the age group of 3-16.

And with time, the number of students started enrolling themselves.

t is an Education NGO that works for education for underprivileged children who are under difficult circumstances,

such as child labor, children of poorest of the parents, children inflicted and affected with HIV/AIDS,

Special emphasis is given on girl education and women’s education so that they and their families get empowered.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Skill Development – It not just what we do as a job, but rather a career, it improves the skill of individual and regulates employment.

According to World Competitiveness Yearbook, 2012, India will have 25% of the total global workforce by about 2025.

As we know, Youth always play a crucial role in achieving the economic growth and prosperity of any country.

Around 688 million Indian population falling under the working-age category,

but most of them remain jobless due to a lack of awareness of the right vocational training and job opportunities.

The major necessity of the time is skill development to reduce the unemployment that generates a strong economy.



WOMEN EMPOWERMENT(SAFE)

Women Empowerment refers to increasing and improving the social, economic, political, and legal strength of the women,

to ensure equal-right to women. Women empowerment helps women to control and benefit from resources,

assets, income, and their own time, as well as the ability to manage risk and improve their economic status and wellbeing.

Many of the barriers to women’s empowerment and equity lie ingrained in cultural norms.

Most women feel the pressures, while others have become accustomed to being treated inferior to men.
