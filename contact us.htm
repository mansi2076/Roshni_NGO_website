OUR PRESENCE

ROSHNI Organization is a powerful catalyst for change in India, operating in Delhi, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Jharkhand, and Rajasthan. With a dedicated focus on child education and women empowerment, ROSHNI is making a tangible difference in the lives of marginalized communities.

Its education initiatives provide quality schooling, bridging the educational gap for underprivileged children. Moreover, ROSHNI empowers women by imparting valuable skills and financial literacy training, enabling them to become self-reliant and independent. By working tirelessly in these states, ROSHNI has become a beacon of hope, offering a brighter future
